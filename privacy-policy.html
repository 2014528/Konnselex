General information

The following information gives you an overview of how your personal data is handled when you visit our website. Personal data refers to any information that can identify you directly or indirectly. More detailed information about data protection and the handling of your data can be found in the privacy policy below.

Data Collection on This Website

Who is responsible for data collection on this website?

The processing of personal data on this website is carried out by the website operator. You can find the contact details of the responsible party in the section titled “Information about the Controller” within this privacy policy.

How do we collect your data?

Some data is collected when you voluntarily provide it to us, for example by filling out a contact form or communicating with us directly.

Additional information is collected automatically by our IT systems or after you have given your consent when visiting the website. This mainly includes technical information such as your browser type, operating system, device information, or the time you accessed the website. This data is collected automatically as soon as you access our website.

Why do we use your data?

Part of the data is used to ensure the proper functionality and security of the website. Other data may be used to better understand user behavior and improve our services and website experience.

What rights do you have regarding your data?

You have the right to request information about the origin, recipients, and purpose of your stored personal data at any time and free of charge. You may also request the correction, restriction, or deletion of your personal data where legally permitted. If you have previously given consent for data processing, you may withdraw that consent at any time with future effect. In addition, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority if you believe your data protection rights have been violated. If you have any questions regarding data protection or the processing of your personal data, you are welcome to contact us at any time.

Data Protection

We take the protection of your personal data very seriously and process your information confidentially in accordance with applicable data protection laws and this privacy policy.

When you use this website, certain personal data may be collected. Personal data refers to any information that can identify you directly or indirectly, as defined under Art. 4 No. 1 GDPR. This may include information such as your name, address, telephone number, email address, or IP address.

This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the purpose of such processing.

Please note that data transmission over the internet, for example through email communication, may involve security risks. While we take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, complete protection against unauthorized access by third parties cannot be guaranteed.

Duration of Storage

Unless a specific storage period is stated in this privacy policy, we will retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected.

In such cases, the data will be deleted once the applicable retention period has expired.

General Information on the Legal Basis for Data Processing

If you have given your consent to the processing of your personal data, we process your data on the basis of Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR. Where special categories of personal data are processed, this is carried out in accordance with Art. 9 para. 2 lit. a GDPR. Where the processing of your data is necessary for the performance of a contract or for pre-contractual measures, the legal basis is Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR. We may also process your data where required to comply with legal obligations in accordance with Art. 6 para. 1 lit. c GDPR.

In certain cases, data processing may also be based on our legitimate interests pursuant to Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR. Further details regarding the applicable legal basis for individual cases can be found in the following sections of this privacy policy.

Recipients of Personal Data

As part of our business operations, we may work with external service providers and partners. In certain cases, it may be necessary to share personal data with these parties. We only transfer personal data where this is required to fulfill a contract, comply with legal obligations, protect our legitimate interests in accordance with Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR, or where another legal basis permits such transfer. Where external service providers process personal data on our behalf, this is done exclusively on the basis of a valid data processing agreement. In cases of joint data processing, a joint processing agreement is concluded where required by law. Further information about our service providers and data processors can be requested at any time using the contact details provided in this privacy policy.

Withdrawal of Consent to Data Processing

Certain data processing activities are carried out only with your explicit consent. You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time with future effect. The withdrawal of consent does not affect the legality of any data processing carried out before the withdrawal. You may send your revocation informally using the contact details provided in this privacy policy.

Right to Object to Data Processing and Direct Marketing (Art. 21 GDPR)

If the processing of your personal data is based on Art. 6 para. 1 lit. e or lit. f GDPR, you have the right to object to such processing at any time for reasons arising from your particular situation. This also applies to any profiling based on these provisions. The relevant legal basis for the processing can be found in this privacy policy.

If you submit an objection, we will no longer process your personal data unless we can demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds for the processing that outweigh your interests, rights, and freedoms, or if the processing is necessary for the establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims.

Where your personal data is processed for direct marketing purposes, you also have the right to object at any time to the use of your data for such marketing activities, including any related profiling. Once you object, your personal data will no longer be used for direct marketing purposes.

Right to Data Portability

You have the right to receive the personal data that we process automatically on the basis of your consent or for the performance of a contract in a commonly used, machine-readable format. You may also request that this data be transferred directly to another data controller, it can only take place if it is technically feasible.

Information, Correction and Deletion

In accordance with applicable legal provisions, you have the right to request free information at any time about your stored personal data, including its origin, recipients, and the purpose of the processing. You also have the right to request the correction or deletion of your personal data where legally permitted. If you have any questions regarding your personal data or your rights under data protection law, you may contact us at any time.

Right to Restriction of Processing

You have the right to request the restriction of the processing of your personal data under certain circumstances. You may contact us at any time to exercise this right.

The right to restriction of processing applies in particular in the following cases:

If you believe that the personal data we hold about you is inaccurate, you may request a restriction of processing while we verify the accuracy of the data.

If the processing of your personal data is unlawful, you may request restriction of processing instead of deletion of the data.

If we no longer require your personal data, but you need it for the establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims, you may request restriction of processing instead of deletion.

If you have objected to processing pursuant to Art. 21 para. 1 GDPR, processing may be restricted while a balance is made between your interests and ours.

Where the processing of your personal data has been restricted, such data may, apart from storage, only be processed with your consent or for the establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims, for the protection of the rights of another person, or for reasons of important public interest under European Union or Member State law.

SSL or TLS Encryption

For security reasons and to protect the transmission of confidential information, this website uses SSL or TLS encryption. This applies, for example, when you send inquiries, orders, or other information to us through the website. You can recognize a secure encrypted connection when the website address changes from “http://” to “https://” and a lock symbol appears in your browser’s address bar. When SSL or TLS encryption is enabled, the data you transmit to us is protected and cannot be accessed by unauthorized third parties.
